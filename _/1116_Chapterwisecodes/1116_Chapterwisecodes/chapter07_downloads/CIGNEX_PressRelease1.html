Two of CIGNEX's thought leaders will be speaking at Transformation and Innovation 2006 (Washington DC), Collaborative Technologies Conference (Boston, MA) and JBoss World (Las Vegas NV).

Navin V. Learn more at http://openlogue.com/ - where he blogs his perspective on topics ranging like patents, open source software adoption, economics of open source software, etc. For more details on the event, please visit: http://www.transformationandinnovation.com/pages/agenda.php

Munwar Shariff, CTO and VP of Business Development at CIGNEX shall be speaking on "Open Source Collaboration Architecture for the Enterprise - Can It Really Be Done?" at the Collaborative Technologies Conference in Boston on June 20th, 2006. Munwar is a renowned expert in Enterprise Content Management Systems with extensive expertise in Plone, Alfresco, JBPM and Zope technologies. About CIGNEX

With more than 50 successful open source deployments, CIGNEX (http://www.cignex.com) leads the industry in providing open source consulting services and software solutions for business. CIGNEX customers include companies in education, healthcare, government and high tech, such as Lawrence Livermore National Laboratories, Epson Electronics America, University of North Carolina Healthcare, University of North Carolina School of Medicine and OKI Semiconductor. CIGNEX is privately held and based in Santa Clara, Calif. The company was founded in 2000 and has been profitable since inception.
